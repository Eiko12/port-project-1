Ali's Achievements

Olympic Medalist

Ali won his first three fights at the Olympics with two unanimous decisions and a second-round knockout. In the final, he faced three-time European champion Zbigniew Pietrzykowski of Poland. After being dominated by his opponent in the first two rounds, Ali found top form in the third round mauling his opponent and almost securing a knock-out. Ali was declared winner by all the judges making him the light heavyweight Olympic champion.

Heavyweight Champion

Muhammad Ali was the first man to win the World Heavyweight title 3 times. Ali won twenty-two World Heavyweight Championship fights and had 19 successful title defences, 9 during his first reign as champion and 10 during his second reign.

The Peoples Champion

He was a prominent humanitarian and activist. Post retirement, Ali dedicated his time to helping others, He donated millions of dollars to charity organizations. It is estimated that he helped provide food for more than 22 million people affected by hunger in the world.
